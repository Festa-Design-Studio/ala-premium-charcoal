Usage Guidelines

  • Use gradients strategically for hero sections, prominent backgrounds, and call-to-action containers
  • Ensure proper contrast for text readability when placing text on gradients
  • Maintain the professional appearance of our B2B-focused interface
  • Consider accessibility requirements when combining text with gradient backgrounds

Accessibility Considerations

When working with our color palette, ensure your designs meet WCAG 2.1 accessibility standards:

Good Combinations

Aa
Charcoal-700 background with Clay-50 text
Aa
Palm-500 background with Clay-50 text
Aa
Clay-50 background with Charcoal-700 text

Combinations to Avoid

Aa
Low contrast: Clay-100 with Ash-300 text
Aa
Low contrast: Palm-300 with Palm-600 text
Aa
Low contrast: Charcoal-200 with Charcoal-400 text

Always aim for a minimum contrast ratio of 4.5:1 for normal text and 3:1 for large text to ensure readability for all users.
